# Spacesio Beryl - User Manual

This manual provides a step-by-step guide to using the Spacesio Beryl ERP & Field Service software from start to finish.

## 1. Authentication & Ghost Mode
**Login:**
1. Open the application on your web browser or mobile device.
2. Enter your Email and Password (e.g., `admin@gmail.com` and `admin123`).
3. Click "Log In".

**PIN Verification:**
* If this is your first time logging in, you will be prompted to set up a **Normal PIN** (for standard access) and a **High-Security PIN** (for Ghost Mode).
* **Ghost Mode:** If you enter your High-Security PIN, sensitive financial records (like cash payments and specific pricing fields) will be hidden across the application. 

## 2. HR & Administration
* **Attendance:** Click "Check-In" when you start work and "Check-Out" when you finish. The system verifies your IP address.
* **Leaves:** Go to the "Leaves" section to submit a time-off request. Management can approve or reject leaves from the "Admin Leaves" portal.
* **Expenses:** Go to "My Expenses", tap the "+" button, upload a photo of your receipt, and enter the amount.

## 3. CRM & Sales
* **Leads:** Click on "CRM" to view leads. Create a new lead to track a potential client.
* **Status Updates:** Keep the lead status updated (New -> First Call -> PDF Sent -> Negotiation -> Finalized). If a lead is lost, you must provide a reason.
* **Quotations:** Open a Lead to create a Quotation. Add line items and payment terms. 
* **Approval:** Once the client agrees, click "Approve" on the quotation. This automatically creates an Order in the Logistics module.

## 4. Logistics & Supply Chain
* **Orders:** Navigate to "Logistics". Approved CRM quotations appear here as Orders.
* **Purchase Orders:** Expand an order to create a Purchase Order (PO) to vendors for materials.
* **Dispatch:** Schedule a delivery dispatch, assign a driver and a vehicle.
* **Delivery Logging:** Drivers can use the "My Dispatches" tab to log when they leave the warehouse and when they deliver to the site. 

## 5. Execution & Field Service
* **Installation Jobs:** Once logistics delivers materials, an Installation Job is created.
* **Assign Installer:** Technical Managers can select a verified contractor from the "Installers" directory and assign them to the job with a set budget.
* **Check In / Out:** Managers log the contractor's daily presence on site.
* **Site Updates:** Capture photos and notes of the work progress. (These can be synced later if you are offline).
* **Payments & Financial Lock:** You can issue advance payments. However, you **cannot** issue the `final_discharge` payment until the client has provided a physical signoff.
* **Signoff:** Have the client sign the tablet. Once approved, the final payment is unlocked.
